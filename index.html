Show Overview

The Bad Mom Podcast, hosted by humanitarian and parent advocate Vivian Glyck, exposes the hidden systems driving today's anxious generation. Through fearless, research-backed conversations with leading experts and cultural changemakers, Vivian delivers clarity, compassion, and actionable tools for parents who are done with blame culture and ready for truth.

This is where cultural leaders and parent-forward brands go to have the important conversations no one else is having.

About the Host — Vivian Glyck

Founder of the Just Like My Child Foundation

Global humanitarian · Parent advocate · Speaker

For two decades, Vivian has led programs that have empowered more than 200,000 adolescent girls and transformed family systems worldwide. She brings unmatched depth, honesty, and lived experience to every conversation—creating an environment where guests go deeper and listeners feel seen, validated, and equipped.
